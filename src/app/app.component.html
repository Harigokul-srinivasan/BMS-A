About

Harley-Davidson, Inc. ( H-D, or simply Harley) is an American motorcycle
manufacturer headquartered in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, United States.
Founded in 1903, it is one of two major American motorcycle manufacturers
to survive the Great Depression along with its historical rival,
Indian Motorcycles.
